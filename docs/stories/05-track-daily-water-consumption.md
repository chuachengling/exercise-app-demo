# User Story: 5 - Track Daily Water Consumption

**As a** user,
**I want** to record my daily water intake,
**so that** I can ensure I'm staying properly hydrated and track this aspect of my health.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Users can quickly log water consumption throughout the day
* Interface allows for easy entry of different water amounts (glasses, bottles, liters)
* Daily water intake is displayed with progress toward recommended daily goals
* Water tracking data integrates with the main health dashboard
* Historical water intake data can be viewed over time
* All water consumption data is saved locally

## Notes

* Should provide a simple, fast interface since water tracking happens frequently throughout the day
* Consider preset amounts for quick logging (8oz glass, 16oz bottle, etc.)
