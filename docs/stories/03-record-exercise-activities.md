# User Story: 3 - Record Exercise Activities

**As a** user,
**I want** to record my exercise activities in the app,
**so that** I can track my fitness progress and maintain a comprehensive health diary.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Users can access an exercise tracking page from the main navigation
* Users can log different types of exercises (HIIT, weightlifting, cardio, etc.)
* Each exercise entry captures relevant details (duration, type, intensity, notes)
* Users can record exercises both during and after their workout
* All exercise data is saved locally to the device
* Previously recorded exercises can be viewed and edited

## Notes

* Should support various exercise types mentioned: HIIT, weightlifting, arms-focused sets
* Flexible timing allows users to log during or after workouts based on preference
