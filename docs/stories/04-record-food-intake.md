# User Story: 4 - Record Food Intake

**As a** user,
**I want** to record the food I eat throughout the day,
**so that** I can track my nutritional intake and maintain awareness of my eating habits.

## Acceptance Criteria

* Users can access a food tracking page from the main navigation
* Users can log individual meals and snacks with relevant details
* Food entries include portion sizes and timing information
* Users can search for common foods or add custom entries
* All food data is saved locally to the device
* Users can view their daily, weekly, or monthly food intake history

## Notes

* This is part of the comprehensive health diary functionality
* Should integrate with the dashboard for overall health tracking visualization
